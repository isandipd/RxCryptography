The Rijndael

The Rijndael is a cryptography meet-up or conference for technology executives, strategy leaders and cybersecurity practitioners. Although cryptography is widely used, it is very poorly understood. The Rijndael strives to bridge this gap.

The objective of The Rijndael is to foster the growth and development of cryptography, digital privacy, and data security for the betterment of society. The Rijndael will provide an interactive and informative platform to increase awareness, promote the latest advancements in research, set a stage for presenting new ideas, and facilitate networking and collaboration among academia, industry, and government.

Key Features

  • Keynote speeches by prominent figures in the industry.
  • Technical paper presentations
  • Panel discussions on emerging trends
  • Hands-on workshops, table-top exercises and tutorials

Highlights of Discussion

  • Post-quantum cryptography
  • Homomorphic Encryption
  • Blockchain and decentralized systems
  • Ransomware
  • Privacy-preserving technologies
  • AI and Cryptography
  • Lightweight cryptography
