The Rijndael

The Rijndael is a cryptography and digital privacy meet-up/conference for technology executives, strategy leaders, cybersecurity practitioners, privacy advocates, and cryptography enthusiasts.

Although cryptography is widely used in our daily lives, information security professionals haven't embraced the subject. The Rijndael strives to bridge this gap by helping cybersecurity professionals understand cryptography and its implementation challenges

Mission

The Rijndael's mission is to foster the growth and development of cryptography, digital privacy, and data security for the betterment of society. The Rijndael will provide an interactive and informative platform to increase awareness, promote the latest advancements in research, set a stage for presenting new ideas, and facilitate networking and collaboration among academia, industry, and government.

Key Features

  • Keynote speeches by prominent figures in the industry.
  • Technical paper presentations
  • Panel discussions on emerging trends
  • Hands-on workshops, table-top exercises and tutorials

Highlights of Discussion

  • Post-quantum cryptography
  • Homomorphic Encryption
  • Blockchain and decentralized systems
  • Ransomware
  • Privacy-preserving technologies
  • AI and Cryptography
  • Lightweight cryptography
