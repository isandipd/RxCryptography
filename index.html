The Rijndael

The Rijndael is a cryptography and digital privacy meet-up/conference for technology executives, strategy leaders, cybersecurity practitioners, privacy advocates, and cryptography enthusiats.

Although cryptography is widely used in our daily life, information security professionals haven't embraced the subject. The Rijndael strives to bridge this gap by helping cybersecurity profesionals understand cryptography and its implementation challenges
