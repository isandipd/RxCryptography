The Rijndael

The Rijndael is a cryptography meet-up or conference for technology execuritves, strategy leaders and cybersecurity practitioners.

The objective of The Rijndael is to foster the growth and development of cryptography, digital privacy, and data security for the betterment of society. The Rijndael will provide an interactive and informative platform to increase awareness, promote the latest advancements in research, set a stage for presenting new ideas, and facilitate networking and collaboration among academia, industry, and government.
